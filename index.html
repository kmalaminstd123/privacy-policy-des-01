Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

  • Directly from you when you fill out forms, register for programs, or contact us.
  • Automatically through cookies and similar technologies when you browse our website.
  • Indirectly from third-party sources, if applicable.

The types of personal data we may collect include your name, email address, contact details, academic information, and usage data such as IP address and browser type.

2. Use of Personal Information

  • Provide and improve our educational services.
  • Communicate with you about programs, updates, or inquiries.
  • Personalize your experience on our website.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our website from misuse.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights. If we use third-party service providers, we ensure they follow similar privacy standards.

5. Data Security and Retention

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, object to or restrict certain processing, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at info@mba-edu.uk.

Refund and Fee Adjustment Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2025

World Academy maintains a strict and transparent refund and fee adjustment policy to ensure clarity and fairness for all participants. By enrolling in any World Academy program, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

  • Non-Refundable Fees: All deposited fees are generally non-refundable. However, if you notify World Academy in writing within seven (7) days of your enrollment, you may request to adjust your fees toward another program offered by World Academy. If you choose to withdraw completely from all programs, World Academy reserves the right to accept or reject your request for a refund or adjustment at its sole discretion.
  • Refund Requests: To request a refund, participants must submit a formal written request to info@higher-education.us within seven (7) days of the program start date, clearly stating the reason for the request. Please note that a refund processing fee will be deducted from any approved refund amount.
  • Course Cancellation: In the event that a course is cancelled by World Academy, participants will receive a full refund, which will be processed within thirty (30) days.
  • Partial Refunds: If a participant has completed up to 10% of the course after enrollment, a partial refund may be issued. Requests made after exceeding 10% course completion, or after receiving a grade of "Pass" or better, will not be eligible for a refund.
  • Digital Materials: All digital materials, including course content and resources, are strictly non-returnable and non-refundable once accessed or downloaded by the participant.
  • Processing Timeline: Approved refunds will be processed within thirty (30) days. For payments made by credit card, refunds will be credited to the same card within this period.
